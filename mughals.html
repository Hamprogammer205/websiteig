The Mughal Empire

The Mughal Empire was established in the year 1526 after Babur defeated Ibrahim Lodhi in the first battle of Panipat.The Empire rule from early 16th to the mid-18th century.The empire were descendents of the Timurid dynasty and Mongols although they were not proud of their Mongolian ancestors ancestors and always showcased their Timurid ancestors.The major rulesrs of this empire were the first six rulers that were:

  1. Babur
  2. Humayun
  3. Akbar
  4. Jahangir
  5. Shah Jahan
  6. Aurangzeb

The greatest ruler among them was Akbar and the Mughal empire was at its geographical peak during the reign of Aurangzeb.After Aurangzeb there were multiple rulers but they were not very powerful and many regional empires came out of the Mughal Empire.The last Mughal Empire that is Bahadur Shah Zafar II was also chosen to lead the 1856 revolt after which he was captured and sent to Rangoon Jail by the britishers and the Governor General stated that any descendant of the Mughal Empire would now be called princes and not kings.
